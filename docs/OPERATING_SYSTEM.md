# Operating System — Yirra NDIS Referral Engine

## Objective

Generate qualified referral conversations for Yirra Care from Support Coordinators, Recovery Coaches, OTs, Plan Managers and community organisations.

## What matters

Track money metrics, not vanity metrics:

- Contacts found
- Qualified contacts
- Drafts created
- Emails sent manually
- Replies
- Positive replies
- Referrals received
- Clients onboarded
- Weekly revenue created

## Best first campaign

**Campaign:** NDIS Cleaning Capacity  
**Target:** Support Coordinators  
**Area:** Brisbane North + Moreton Bay  
**Offer:** Current capacity for reliable NDIS cleaning and domestic tasks  
**Goal:** 3 referrals from 100 quality contacts

## Daily workflow

1. Find 20–30 new contacts.
2. Scrape/enrich.
3. Qualify.
4. Generate drafts.
5. Greg reviews drafts.
6. Send only clean, relevant emails.
7. Log replies manually.

## Do not do this

- Do not send hundreds per day.
- Do not use generic agency-style emails.
- Do not pretend you know the person if you do not.
- Do not scrape LinkedIn aggressively.
- Do not send from a domain you cannot afford to damage.

## Priority contacts

1. Support Coordinators
2. Recovery Coaches
3. OTs
4. Community organisations
5. Plan Managers

Plan managers are not first priority because they usually process invoices, not referrals.
